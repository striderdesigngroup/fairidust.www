Tea Leaf Reading Magical Herbs
 

Ever wonder what your future holds from the inside of a tea cup? Tea has been around for centuries with its origins from Asia. From the birth of tea, tea leaf readers ensued.

Tasseography is tea leaf reading. It means Tassa, the root, is the Arabic word for cup. The popularity of tea leaf reading has been popular even when coffee was introduced. If your like most when you think of someone getting a reading you envision a traveling gypsy in a mystical wagon, or a dark roomed veiled psychic.

Tea reading enthusiasts ca be found everywhere and you can try it yourself. The following is a brief description of how you can try it at home.

You will need:

1) A wide plain white tea cup with saucer (no mugs)

2) Hot boiling water for tea

3) Good quality black tea

Note: English teas are the best. Do not use tea bags. A quality tea is free of debris that can interfere with your reading.

Now your ready to state, so brew your tea, let it steep, and enjoy your beverage. Leave about a slight teaspoon of the liquid at the bottom of the cup.

Next step, swish it 3 times counter clockwise and slowly turn over onto saucer to let the remaining liquid to drip out. Now your ready to read your cup.

When looking into your cup, note that the leaves that are near the top mean events are approaching soon. Leaves on the sides are the not so distant future and those on the bottom are the far future.

Symbolism of the leaves are not exact. Try to feel for the flow of the symbols to help direct you towards a pattern.
